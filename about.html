Our Story

A passion for craft, a devotion to elegance, and a belief that the perfect hat can transform how you feel.

From Passion to Atelier

Founded in 2015 in the heart of New York City, Atelier was born from a simple belief: that a beautifully crafted hat has the power to elevate not just an outfit, but a moment. What began as a small workshop above a vintage clothing store has grown into one of the most respected boutique millineries in the country.

Every hat that leaves our atelier carries our founding promise — uncompromising craftsmanship, exceptional materials, and a design that feels both timeless and distinctly personal.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped who we are today.

2015

Atelier founded in a small Manhattan workshop with just three hat blocks and a dream.

2017

First feature in Vogue — "The New Milliners to Watch." Orders tripled overnight.

2019

Opened our flagship boutique on Fashion Avenue with a full atelier and showroom.

2021

Launched the Custom Hat Builder — bringing bespoke design to the digital experience.

2024

Over 5,000 custom hats crafted. Partnerships with leading bridal designers worldwide.

Meet Our Designer

Isabelle Moreau — Founder & Lead Milliner

With over 15 years of experience in haute couture millinery, Isabelle trained under legendary hat-makers in Paris and London before founding Atelier. Her designs seamlessly blend traditional craftsmanship with a modern editorial eye.

Her work has been worn by celebrities, featured at royal events, and exhibited in fashion museums. Yet her greatest joy remains that moment when a client tries on their custom hat for the first time and their face lights up.

"A hat isn't just an accessory — it's an attitude. When you find the right one, you don't just wear it. You become it."

Our Craft Process

Every hat goes through seven meticulous stages of creation.

Design

Sketching and planning the hat's silhouette, proportions and details.

Material Selection

Choosing the perfect felt, straw, or fabric from our curated collection.

Blocking

Shaping the hat on traditional wooden blocks using steam and pressure.

Atelier

Bespoke millinery crafted with passion and precision. Each hat is a unique work of art.
